Automate #YangGang Event Tweets

Mobilize volunteers to upcoming events for Andrew Yang via Twitter!

Created by San Francisco Yang Gang (@sfyanggang).

Used by Arizona, Cincinatti, Florida, Houston, Illinois, Las Vegas, Maine, Massachusetts, New Jersey, New Orleans, New York, Massachusetts, New England, Oakland, San Francisco Yang Gangs.

Automatically tweets Yang volunteer events in your region from Mobilize.

Once you authorize your Twitter account, I will manually configure and enable it to run for events in your region. Events will be tweeted on a regular schedule (up to three times per event leading up to the event). Contact @bubblepoptarts for questions and requests!
